title: "Blocking of voter registration abroad"
afirmacion: "The National Electoral Council (CNE) imposed arbitrary and technical hurdles that prevented millions of Venezuelans abroad from registering to vote."
norma: "Organic Law: All Venezuelans over 18 have the right to register and vote, regardless of their residence."
evidencia: "Official CNE requirement lists for residence status, short registration windows, and lack of consular capacity."
impacto: "More than 4 million Venezuelans of voting age in the diaspora (per international estimates) were effectively unable to register or vote, affecting the universality and legitimacy of the process."
replica: "The CNE cited 'logistical constraints' and existing laws for residency requirements."
